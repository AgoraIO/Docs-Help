Agora is a Platform as a Service (PaaS) provider that enables real-time communicatons with easy-to-embed SDKs and RESTful APIs. Through calling the APIs, you can add the following functions in your applications:

- [Video Calling](https://docs.agora.io/en/video-calling/overview/product-overview)
- [Interactive Live Streaming](https://docs.agora.io/en/interactive-live-streaming/overview/product-overview)
- [Voice Calling](https://docs.agora.io/en/voice-calling/overview/product-overview)
- [Chat (Beta)](https://docs.agora.io/en/agora-chat/overview/product-overview)
- [Signaling](https://docs.agora.io/en/signaling/overview/product-overview)
- [Interactive Whiteboard](https://docs.agora.io/en/interactive-whiteboard/overview/product-overview)
- [Analytics](https://docs.agora.io/en/agora-analytics/overview/product-overview)
- [Cloud Recording](https://docs.agora.io/en/cloud-recording/overview/product-overview)
- [On-premise Recording](https://docs.agora.io/en/on-premise-recording/overview/product-overview)
- [Server Gateway](https://docs.agora.io/en/server-gateway/overview/product-overview)
- [Flexible classroom](https://docs.agora.io/en/flexible-classroom/overview/product-overview)
- [Media Push](https://docs.agora.io/en/media-push/overview/product-overview)
- [Media Pull](https://docs.agora.io/en/media-pull/overview/product-overview)
